WISDOM

Research Survey Questionnaire

Welcome to the WISDOM questionnaire, a research tool designed to assess wisdom-related traits and behaviors. This survey consists of 20 carefully crafted questions about your thoughts, attitudes, and approaches to learning and decision-making.

What to Expect:

  • Time Required: Approximately 5-10 minutes
  • Questions: 20 statements with 5-point response scale
  • Topics: Creativity, curiosity, judgment, and social wisdom
  • Privacy: Your responses are confidential and used for research purposes only

Your participation contributes to important research in understanding wisdom and its various dimensions. There are no right or wrong answers - we're interested in your honest responses about how well each statement describes you.
